About Us

AstroPhoenix - Team

Meet our fantastic team of 5 dedicated members, all coming together to document objects near Earth and present them in an engaging and interactive manner that's easy for everyone to understand. We're on a mission to create a virtual orrery that is not only easy to explore but also interactive, aiming to push the boundaries in documenting objects near Earth. Our project is all about being interactive, visually captivating, and educational, and we can't wait to showcase our passion for this exciting event.
